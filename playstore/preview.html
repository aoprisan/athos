IV

SPLASH

ΦΩΣ

The first frame. Square 2732 × 2732 so Capacitor's CENTER_CROP behaves on every device — phone portrait, phone landscape, tablet. Bleed color matches the Capacitor config.

Capacitor Splash

2732 × 2732 · square · bleed #0f1a16

Drops into android/app/src/main/res/drawable*/splash.png and ios/App/App/Assets.xcassets/Splash.imageset/. The mandorla sits dead center so device safe areas don't crop the cross.

EXPORT

ΓΡΑΦΗ

One command, every PNG. Uses rsvg-convert (sharp, fast, faithful) or falls back to ImageMagick.
