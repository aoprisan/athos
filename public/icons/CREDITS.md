# Sky-band icon credits

The two images in this directory are faithful photographic reproductions of
medieval Byzantine artworks. The underlying artworks are in the public domain
under any reasonable jurisdiction by virtue of age. Per *Bridgeman Art Library
v. Corel Corp.* (1999) and EU/UK 2D-reproduction practice, faithful
photographic reproductions of public-domain 2D artworks do not generate new
copyright.

- **theotokos.jpg** — *Our Lady of the Sign* (Богоматерь Знамение), Novgorod
  icon-painting school, early replica of an 11th-century original.
  Tretyakov Gallery, Russia.
  Source: <https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Icon_of_Our_Lady_of_the_Sign._Novgorod_icon-painting_school,_early_replica_of_11th_cent.original._Tretyakov_galley,_Russia.jpg>

- **archangel.jpg** — *Archangel* (Mikharkhangel), 13th-century Byzantine icon.
  Source: <https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Mikharkhangel.jpg>
